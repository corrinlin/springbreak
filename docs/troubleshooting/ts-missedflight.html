Missed Flight

What to do if you've missed your first flight.

You've missed the flight from Norfolk.

You were late to the airport.

It doesn't matter if there was too much traffic, or if you took too long at home, or if you left Blacksburg too late. It was your fault you missed your flight.

You and the airline employee will need to work together.

  • Go to the Delta check-in counter.
  • Tell them you've missed your flight to Atlanta and tell them you're going to Chicago. Ask them if they have anymore outgoing flights that can get you to Chicago.
  • Buy a new plane ticket.
  • Unless the flight is the next morning, don't leave the airport.
